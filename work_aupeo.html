Aupeo!

2015

Aupeo! lets you create your own personal podcast mixes to listen to during the day.

With Aupeo! we used the Mirror-Link technology that allows plugged-in smartphones to send custom layouts to the car embedded screen, so that functions and elements can be best styled for usage while driving.

UX/UI Design, Specification
