Smart Home

2016

Today, devices of all kind get smarter and smarter, informations and features get more complex, and the need for strong design guidelines gets higher. consistency and brand recognisability.

As a designer I worked with the client and the team on two levels: co-creation of features that would improve the user experience, and definition of general styles, patterns and elements that could be applied to all appliances, to ensure consistency and brand recognisability.
