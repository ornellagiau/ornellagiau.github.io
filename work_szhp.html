For Süddeutsche Zeitung

2023-2024

Digital transformation is crucial in the Publishing industry. Content consumers are jumping from platform to platform, increasingly digital. Optimizing for printout is just not going to cut it anymore.

Since 2020 I have been working on Lux, the new, shiny CMS Software for the Süeddeutsche Zeitung. I wanted to offer the editorial team a modern interface to compose their stories, as well as support the digital transformation.

At the moment the CMS environment consistes of two editor softwares, one that is more channel-neutral and one that is compleatly dedicated to optimizing stories for online readers.

Role
Concept, Design Lead, Implementation support, User research, Product Discovery
